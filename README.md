# HR Analytics Dashboard using PowerBI for Employee Attrition

Welcome to the **HR Analytics Dashboard** for analyzing employee attrition in the firm. This dashboard provides comprehensive insights into various factors affecting attrition using data from our HR analytics dataset.

## Overview

This dashboard was created using **Power BI** to visualize and analyze the attrition trends within the company. The dataset contains multiple columns essential for our analysis, including employee demographics, job roles, salaries, and more.

## Preprocessing Steps

Before developing the dashboard, I performed essential preprocessing steps, such as:

- Cleaning and transforming data
- Handling missing values
- Creating new features like **Attrition by Age**

## Key Metrics and Visualizations

The dashboard addresses the problem of employee attrition with the following metrics and visualizations:

- **Total Employees**: The total number of employees in the firm.
- **Attrition Count**: The number of employees who have left the firm.
- **Attrition Rate**: The percentage of employees who have left the firm.
- **Average Employee Age**: The average age of employees.
- **Average Retention Period**: The average duration employees stay with the firm.
- **Average Salary**: The average salary of employees.

### Visualizations

- **Attrition by Education**: A chart showing attrition rates segmented by education levels.
- **Attrition by Age**: A chart illustrating attrition rates across different age groups.
- **Attrition by Salary**: A chart comparing attrition rates with employee salaries.
- **Attrition by Years at Company**: A chart depicting attrition rates based on the number of years employees have been with the company.
- **Attrition by Job Role**: A chart highlighting attrition rates across various job roles.
- **Job Satisfaction Table**: A table showcasing job satisfaction scores among employees.

### Filters

- **Department Filter**: Major filter based on the department, allowing insights specific to:
  - Human Resources
  - Research & Development
  - Sales

## Conclusion

This dashboard provides valuable insights into the factors influencing employee attrition, helping the company develop effective strategies to improve retention and overall employee satisfaction.
